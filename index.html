About me

Hello! My name is Sean Maloney, I am an Undergraduate student in the Department of Physics and Astronomy as the University of Pittsburgh. I study the foundations of astronomy from stellar to galactic astrophysics as well as the fundamentals of modern physics. I have shaped my learning and also have gotten to explore more specific interests through my research opportunities and have found a particular passion for cosmology.
Though I define myself as a cosmology enthusiast, I have never boxed myself in and have explored many different sides of physics through my research. My research includes cosmological astrometry, particle / cosmic ray detectors, and modeling various physicsal systems from stars to superconductors.
